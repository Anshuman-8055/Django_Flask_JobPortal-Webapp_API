Resume Writing Tips

Essential Resume Sections

  • Contact Information: Full name, phone, professional email, location, LinkedIn profile
  • Professional Summary: 2-3 sentences highlighting your key qualifications
  • Work Experience: Recent relevant positions with accomplishments
  • Education: Degrees, certifications, and relevant coursework
  • Skills: Technical, soft, and industry-specific skills

Writing Effective Bullet Points

Formula: Action Verb + Task + Result

Weak: "Responsible for social media marketing"

Strong: "Increased social media engagement by 150% through targeted content strategies and community management"

Recommended Action Verbs:

Developed Implemented Orchestrated Streamlined Spearheaded

Formatting Best Practices

  • Use consistent formatting throughout
  • Choose a clean, professional font (Arial, Calibri, or Helvetica)
  • Keep font size between 10-12 points
  • Maintain margins between 0.5" and 1"
  • Limit resume to 1-2 pages

Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Generic Objectives

Replace with targeted professional summaries

❌ Dense Paragraphs

Use bullet points for better readability

❌ Spelling Errors

Proofread multiple times
